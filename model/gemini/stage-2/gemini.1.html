2 Word Problem

A train leaves the station at 9:15 am. The journey takes 90 minutes. What time does the train arrive?

Answer 10:45 am
90 minutes is equal to 1 hour and 30 minutes.
Add 1 hour to 9:15 am → 10:15 am.
Add remaining 30 minutes to 10:15 am → 10:45 am.
3 Algebra

Consider the equation: 2x + 4y = 20

a) Find the value of x when y = 2.

Answer x = 6
Substitute y = 2 into the equation:
2x + 4(2) = 20
2x + 8 = 20
Subtract 8 from both sides: 2x = 12
Divide by 2: x = 6

b) Rearrange the formula to make y the subject.

Answer y = 5 - 0.5x or y = (20 - 2x) / 4

(This is a long derivation example)

Start with: 2x + 4y = 20
Goal: Isolate y.
Subtract 2x from both sides:
4y = 20 - 2x
Divide every term by 4:
y = 20/4 - 2x/4
Simplify the fractions:
y = 5 - 1/2x
Alternative format: y = 5 - 0.5x
Check: If x=6, y = 5 - 3 = 2. Correct.

c) Explain why y decreases as x increases.

Answer Because the coefficient of x is negative.
Looking at the equation y = 5 - 0.5x
The gradient is -0.5.
A negative gradient means the line slopes downwards.

5 Probability

A bag contains 3 red balls and 2 blue balls. What is the probability of picking a blue ball?

Answer 2/5 (or 0.4 or 40%)
Total balls = 3 (red) + 2 (blue) = 5.
Blue balls = 2.
Probability = (Target Outcomes) / (Total Outcomes)
P(Blue) = 2 / 5
